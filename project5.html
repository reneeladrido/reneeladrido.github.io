Ryerson March Nutrition Month

Ryerson Nutrition Month Advertising

Creating advertising posters and digital screens to promote Nutrition Month events at Ryerson.

  • Project Type: Work Assignment
  • Completed: March 2019
  • Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

This project involved developing the promotional material for a series of events held by Ryerson Eats foodservices to celebrate Nutrition Month. The goal was to create eye-catching material that would attract students and increase their interest and awareness of the program in order to attain a larger turnout. We decided to use mounted posters and digital advertising screens in the university retail and residence food areas, depending on what each event was targeting. The result of these promotions were a significant amount of participating students and increased student engagement.


Creative Strategy: I worked with the marketing manager to determine which art direction and theme we should use to best catch the attention of Ryerson students. We also had to consider the fact that our organization was running under Ryerson University and therefore all designs had to adhere to the specific university branding guidelines. The challenge of this project was to think of a way to present the information in a visually appealing way to attract student attention while still maintaining Ryerson brand standards. I also wanted our promotional material to stand out amongst the many other promotions from other university groups that had to use the same branding standards as us. Our solution was to use bright and bold colours and flat-design illustrations throughout all the designs. We chose a bright turquoise blue and yellow from the current Ryerson brand colour guide as our main colour theme and also incorporated the graphic device used in official Ryerson promotions by using the box to emphasize our titles and subtitles. I designed all the illustrations myself using Illustrator to represent each event and to quickly give students an idea of what it is about.
