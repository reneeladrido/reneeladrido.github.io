Peak App

Peak - The Outdoor Adventure App

A mobile app concept designed to connect users to nearby locations and activities to help plan their next outdoor adventure.

  • Project Type: Personal Project
  • Completed: April 2019
  • Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe XD, Adobe Photoshop

This was a personal project challenging myself to come up with an app idea and design the interface and experience for it. I decided to create PEAK, a mobile app that aims to connect outdoor enthusiasts to nearby locations that match their interests and assist them in planning and scheduling those adventures.


Creative Strategy: For the colour theme, I chose this specific palette as the colours are reminiscent of nature and the outdoors (blue skies/seas, green forests, red-orange sunsets) while still maintaining a modern look for the app. Clean line icons, SF font, and simplistic flat design illustrations throughout give the app a modern and professional feeling so that users can trust it. I incorporated illustrations of mountain landscapes to further inspire the feeling of wanting to go exploring outdoors for an adventure.


When thinking about the user experience, I wanted to achieve a system that was easy to use and that made the process of planning a trip easier and quicker so that they would have more time to spend actually experiencing that trip. I included features such as a map and location tracker that shows nearby experiences in their area using information such as their current location, trip history, tailored searching, site information and ratings, and a friend and chat system that allows users to easily send invites and plan their adventures in collaboration with others.
