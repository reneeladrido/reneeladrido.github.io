Chartwells Local Partner Certificate Illustration

Certified Chartwells Local Partner Illustration

Creating the illustration to be displayed on the certificate recognizing local farm suppliers.

  • Project Type: Client Project
  • Client: Chartwells
  • Completed: May 2019
  • Tools used: Adobe Illustrator, Adobe InDesign

Creative Strategy: For this project, I was given the task of creating an illustration to be featured on the Chartwells Local Partner Certificate. This certificate is to be given to local farmers who partner with schools and other organizations under Chartwells as a sign of appreciation for providing fresh, local foods for their food services. It was requested that the illustration be representative of this. I provided several concepts and illustration styles to the client, and it was decided that a colourful farm scene showcasing local produce would be the most ideal for the certificate. A limited red-orange and green palette was used as the main colour theme for the illustration as it was requested that I match the Chartwells Buy Local logo colours as much as possible. I used a simplistic, flat-design illustration style as approved by the client. I also did research for common and popular local produce and incorporated carrots, lettuce, tomatoes, and apples into the illustration (which I also chose as they matched the colour palette).
